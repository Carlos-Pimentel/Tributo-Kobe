Kobe Bryant

Kobe Bean Bryant was an American professional basketball player. A shooting guard, he spent his entire 20-year career with the Los Angeles Lakers in the National Basketball Association.

Kobe Bryant the legend.

This is a little of Kobe's story

  • 2000-NBA Champion
  • 2001-NBA Champion
  • 2002-NBA Champion
  • 2009-NBA Champion
  • 2010-NBA Champion
  • 2009-Named best player in the NBA Finals
  • 2010-Named best player in the NBA Finals
  • 1998,2000-2016-18-time All-League All-Star Game selection
  • 2002,2003,2004,2006-2013-11 times elected to the NBA All-Star Team
  • 4th highest scorer in NBA history
  • Two-time Olympic gold medalist for the USA team
  • 2018-Oscar winner for the short film "Dear Basketball", based on a poem he wrote

"If you want to be great in a particular area, you have to obsess over it. A lot of people say they want to be great, but they're not willing to make the sacrifices necessary to achieve greatness. They have other concerns, whether important or not, and they spread themselves out."
